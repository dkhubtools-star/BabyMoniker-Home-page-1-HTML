Mini-Hacks for Calmer Days

When energy peaks, pause together for "butterfly breaths": inhale while raising arms like wings, exhale and fold down. Two minutes resets focus and builds emotional regulation.

1 Stand together, arms at sides
2 Inhale slowly, raise arms like butterfly wings
3 Exhale, fold gently forward
4 Repeat 5 times, notice calm

Keep three toy bins in rotation: one active, two stored. Swap weekly to renew focus and reduce clutter. Kids rediscover "new" favorites and play with deeper concentration.

Offer "rainbow plates" with colorful, textured finger foods. Let kids touch, smell, and explore before tasting. Builds curiosity, vocabulary, and reduces mealtime battles.
